About

Jorge Viñals is a composer and sound artist based in Spain. His work explores the intersection of acoustic and electronic music, creating immersive sonic experiences that challenge traditional boundaries.

With a background in both classical composition and experimental electronic music, Jorge has developed a unique voice that combines traditional instrumentation with cutting-edge technology.
